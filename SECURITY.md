# Security Policy

## Supported Versions

labs-asp is an experimental Nava PBC R&D project. It is not a production system. Only the latest commit on the `main` branch is actively maintained. No security patches will be backported to older versions or tags.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

**Please do not open a public GitHub issue to report a security vulnerability.**

To report a vulnerability, email [security@navapbc.com](mailto:security@navapbc.com) with the subject line `[labs-asp] Security Vulnerability Report`.

### What to Include

Your report should contain:

- **Description** — a clear explanation of the vulnerability and the component or behavior involved
- **Steps to reproduce** — a minimal, reproducible example or step-by-step instructions to trigger the issue
- **Impact assessment** — your understanding of what an attacker could do by exploiting this vulnerability and under what conditions

The more detail you can provide, the faster we can triage and respond.

## Response Timeline

- **Acknowledgment** — we will acknowledge receipt of your report within 5 business days
- **Triage** — we will assess severity and determine next steps within 10 business days of acknowledgment

Because this is a labs/R&D project, remediation timelines will depend on severity and project priorities. We will keep you informed of progress.

## Disclosure Policy

We follow coordinated disclosure. Please give us a reasonable opportunity to investigate and address the issue before any public disclosure. We will work with you to agree on an appropriate disclosure timeline.

If you would like to be credited for your report, let us know in your email and we will include your name (or handle) in any public acknowledgment.
